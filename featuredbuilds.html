Build of the Week

This Week's Spotlight Belongs to:

Posted by Ryan | Featured Builds

Owner: Jake Schnatter

Vehicle: Ferrari F12 Berlinetta

Main Style: OEM+

Favorite Detail: For someone with a Ferrari, Schnatter represents the car community well with how many cool and interesting mods he has done to it.

This week we are highlighting the famous Jake Schnatter and his well recognized Ferrari F12 Berlinetta build. To expand on what I was saying earlier, although Schnatter got a Ferrari, he does not baby it and loves to put very interesting and cool modifications like a Grail exhaust system which just makes the monsterous V12 in the car come to ever-deafening life. This beautiful car might be a little older, but the sound of that engine is so well received in the car community even today plus the tasteful mods Schnatter has chosen for the car keep it looking mostly OEM but still help it give a certain flare that is only proven by its dominant road presence.
